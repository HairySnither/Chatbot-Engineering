Engineering Topics

Materials Science

Materials science involves the study of the properties and applications of materials of construction or manufacture (such as ceramics, metals, polymers, and composites). Understanding materials science is crucial for engineers to select the right materials for their projects.

Mechanical Systems

Mechanical systems include the design and analysis of mechanical components and systems. This includes understanding forces, motion, and energy. Key topics include statics, dynamics, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics.

Electrical Systems

Electrical systems cover the principles of electricity and electronics. This includes understanding circuits, electrical components, and systems. Key topics include circuit theory, electromagnetism, and power systems.

Manufacturing Processes

Manufacturing processes involve the methods and techniques used to produce goods. This includes machining, casting, welding, and additive manufacturing (3D printing). Understanding these processes is essential for designing and creating engineered products.
